About UniLife+

UniLife+ is a centralized digital platform designed to improve campus life for students of the University of Delta, Agbor. It helps students access accurate information, navigate the campus, stay organized, and reduce academic stress.

Key Features

Lecture Timetable

Access up-to-date lecture schedules for all faculties in one place.

Campus Navigation

Find lecture halls, hostels, offices, and important locations easily.

Accommodation Listings

View available hostels and off-campus accommodation options.

Events & Announcements

Stay updated on school events, deadlines, and official announcements.
